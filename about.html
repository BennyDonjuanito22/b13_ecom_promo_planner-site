Our Story

We Built the Promo Planner We Wished Existed

PromoPilot was founded by e-commerce operators who got tired of planning promotions in spreadsheets, missing key sales windows, and scrambling to create campaigns at the last minute. We believe every brand deserves an intelligent promotional strategy — not just the ones with big marketing teams.

Our Mission

Make promotional planning effortless, intelligent, and revenue-focused for every e-commerce brand on the planet.

The Problem We Saw

Most e-commerce brands run promotions reactively. A holiday pops up, competition runs a sale, or revenue dips — and suddenly the team is scrambling to throw together a discount, an email, and a social post. It's stressful, inefficient, and leaves money on the table every single month.

What We Built

PromoPilot uses AI to generate a complete, year-round promotional calendar tailored to your brand's niche, audience, and revenue goals. From holiday campaigns to flash sales and product launches, every promo is planned, briefed, and ready to execute — months in advance.

Where We're Going

We're building toward a future where promotional planning is fully autonomous. AI that doesn't just suggest promos, but creates the copy, schedules the campaigns, and optimizes performance in real time — so you can focus on building great products.
